## Summary

Tract Map No. 43725 reversed the 1980 subdivision (Tract 32977) back to a single parcel "for reversion to acreage purposes." Filed February 26, 1986, it merged all five lots — including Lot 5 (the HOA common area) — into one lot of 1.58 acres. Owner Kenneth K. Wong abandoned all rights of direct vehicular ingress and egress from the lot to Clipper Road. The City Council approved the reversion on February 18, 1986. This is the current legal description for APN 7573-006-024, the parcel now owned by Clipper Development LLC (Ali Vahdani).

## Key Provisions

- **Purpose:** "FOR REVERSION TO ACREAGE PURPOSES"
- **Scope:** Subdivision of Lots 1 thru 5, inclusive, of Tract No. 32977, as per map filed in Book 950, Pages 14 and 15 of Maps, Records of Los Angeles County
- **Result:** All 5 lots (including HOA common Lot 5) merged into single **Lot 1** — 1.58 acres
- **Owner:** Kenneth K. Wong, with Alice V.L. Wong as owner of such interest as inferred by her joinder in the Deed of Trust recorded March 7, 1979 (Instrument No. 79-255540)
- **Street Dedication:** All of Palos Verdes Drive South within or adjacent to this reversion to acreage remains a public street; owner abandoned all rights of direct vehicular ingress and egress from abutting lot to said street
- **Vacation clause:** If any portion of said street within or adjacent to this reversion to acreage is vacated, such vacation terminates the above dedication as to the part vacated
- **Flood Hazard:** Still noted on plat — flood hazard area occupies central portion of the lot
- **Survey Date:** October 1985
- **Engineer:** Darryl E. Dalcin, R.C.E. 27797, Dalcin Cummins Associates (same engineer as Tract 32977)
- **City Council Approval:** February 18, 1986 — approved the attached map and also accepted the dedication of abandonment of the right of ingress and egress as dedicated
- **City Clerk Certification:** February 6, 1986 — all special assessments paid in full
- **City Engineer:** Donald R. Wirth, certified February 6, 1986
- **Notarized:** December 23, 1985 by Darryl E. Dalcin (commission expires August 1, 1987)
- **Basis of Bearings:** N 9 00' 00" W of the centerline of Clipper Road as shown on Tract No. 14649, M.B. 345/23-26

## Significance to WPBCA

This reversion is the pivotal recorded document in the Clipper/Lot H story:

1. **Eliminated the HOA common area.** Tract 32977 created Lot 5 as a mandatory HOA common area with inseparable membership. Tract 43725 merged Lot 5 back into Lot 1, effectively dissolving the association structure that was a condition of the original subdivision.

2. **Question of surviving restrictions.** The 1980 subdivision included City-accepted dedications restricting construction in flood hazard zones and prohibiting residential structures in common areas. Whether those restrictions survived the reversion to acreage is a central legal question.

3. **Same owner, same engineer.** Kenneth K. Wong subdivided in 1980 and reversed in 1986. Darryl E. Dalcin engineered both maps. The reversion happened only 6 years after subdivision — the planned development was never built.

4. **Current chain of title.** The CTC Property Profile confirms the current legal description is "Lot 1, Tract 43725, MB 1063 PG 91&92" — this reversion map is the operative recorded document for the parcel Clipper Development LLC now owns.

5. **Flood hazard persists.** Both the 1980 and 1986 maps show flood hazard designation in the central portion. This designation predates the current developer's ownership by decades.
