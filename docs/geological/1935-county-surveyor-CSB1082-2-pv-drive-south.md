## TRANSCRIPTION NOTES

This is a panoramic-format survey map (extremely wide aspect ratio) depicting the planned alignment of Palos Verdes Drive South through the Portuguese Bend area. The scan resolution makes much of the fine detail -- bearings, distances, curve data, station numbers -- difficult or impossible to read with confidence. The following transcription captures everything discernible at the available resolution. Items marked [?] are illegible or uncertain.

**A higher-resolution scan is strongly recommended** to capture the full survey data (bearings, distances, curve parameters, monument descriptions, and property owner names). The current scan compresses the map's width into a resolution where most text smaller than the title block cannot be reliably read.

### Evidentiary Value for Dual CC&R Theory

1. **Road alignment through Filiorum Parcel 2** -- PV Drive South runs through the ~160-acre interior parcel granted by PV Corp to Filiorum Corporation (Book 10226, Pages 170-174, 1930)
2. **Survey monuments** -- The 1935 monuments cross-reference the 1930 Filiorum deed metes-and-bounds, establishing geographic overlap with 0 Clipper (APN 7573-006-024)
3. **Pre-landslide alignment** -- Predates the 1956 Portuguese Bend landslide by 21 years, recording original topography
