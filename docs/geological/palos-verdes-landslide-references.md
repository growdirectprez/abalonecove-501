# Palos Verdes Peninsula / Portuguese Bend / Abalone Cove
**Landslide, Geology & Land-Use References**
*Exhaustive official source list — Federal, State, County, University & Professional Society documents*
*Last updated: April 2026*

## 1. Federal — U.S. Geological Survey (USGS)

**Woodring, W.P., Bramlette, M.N., and Kew, W.S.W. (1946)**
*Geology and Paleontology of the Palos Verdes Hills, California*.
U.S. Geological Survey Professional Paper 207, 181 p. + geologic map (Plate 1).
**Significance**: The foundational pre-development geologic map and report. Documents the Monterey Formation (bentonite-rich slide planes), ancient landslides, marine terraces, and unstable south-coast slopes based on 1930s-1940s fieldwork. Every later study cites this as the baseline.
**Local copy**: `docs/geological/1946-woodring-usgs-pp207-geology-palos-verdes.pdf`
**DOI**: https://doi.org/10.3133/pp207

**Wong, F.L. (2001)** *Heavy Minerals from Palos Verdes Margin, Southern California*. USGS Open-File Report 01-153.

## 4. Professional Societies — GSA & AEG

**Cooper, J.D. (compiler) (1982)** *Landslides and Landslide Abatement, Palos Verdes Peninsula, Southern California*. Guidebook and Volume, 78th Annual Meeting GSA Cordilleran Section, Field Trip No. 10.
**Significance**: The single most-cited compilation. Contains Ehlig, Ray, Vonder Linden, Bryant, Novak, Lass, Kerwin.
**Local copy**: `docs/geological/1982-ehlig-landslides-guidebook-verbatim.md` (verbatim transcription in progress)
**Physical copy**: PV Library District Local History Room (call 550.6 LANDSLIDES)

**Ehlig, P.L. (1992)** "Evolution, mechanics and mitigation of the Portuguese Bend landslide, Palos Verdes Peninsula, California." In: Pipkin & Proctor (eds.), *Engineering Geology Practice in Southern California*, AEG Special Publication No. 4, pp. 531-553.

**Vonder Linden, Karl (1989)** "The Portuguese Bend landslide." *Engineering Geology*, v. 27, pp. 301-373.
**Significance**: The definitive 73-page mechanics study.
