## Summary

Three-year lease (April 1, 1971 – March 31, 1974) between Filiorum Corporation and Abalone Shore Club for the beach club property at Portuguese Bend. Rent was $30,000/year paid as 60% of club dues income, with Filiorum controlling the bank account through H.E. Benedict's sole signature authority. Filiorum could terminate with 90 days notice at will. The lease included a non-exclusive access license over the green-outlined access property. Signed by Frank A. Vanderlip Jr. and H.E. Benedict for Filiorum, and by two officers for the Club.

## Key Provisions

- **Term:** April 1, 1971 to March 31, 1974 (3 years)
- **Rent:** $30,000 per lease year, paid as 60% of club dues income monthly starting April 10
- **Minimum dues:** $150 per member per lease year
- **Bank control:** All club income deposited in account requiring H.E. Benedict's (or Filiorum designee's) signature for withdrawals
- **Termination:** Filiorum may terminate on 90 days written notice at any time
- **Refund schedule:** Pro-rated member refunds if terminated mid-year (April: $150, declining monthly to March: $3.75)
- **Insurance:** Club must carry $500,000/$1,000,000 liability insurance naming both parties
- **Use restriction:** Recreational beach club only — food service, lifeguard, swimming
- **Default cure period:** 7 days after notice, then Filiorum may re-enter and take possession
- **Access:** Filiorum and public officials may enter at all times; Club may install electric gate but must provide keys
- **Improvements:** Club may remove all improvements (gates, BBQ pits, gangplanks, floats, equipment) before lease termination
- **Assignment:** Club may not assign without Filiorum's written consent
- **Filiorum notice address:** Three Peppertree Drive, Portuguese Bend, California 90274
- **Club notice address:** Post Office #2, Portuguese Bend, California 90274

LEASE AND LICENSE

This indenture of Lease made this 1st day of June, 1971, by and between FILIORUM CORPORATION, a California corporation, herein for convenience called "Filiorum", and ABALONE SHORE CLUB, a California non-profit corporation, herein for convenience called "Club".

WITNESSETH:

WHEREAS, Club wishes to lease from Filiorum and Filiorum is willing to lease to Club at a rental, for a term, on conditions, and subject to termination, all as herein set forth, certain property in the County of Los Angeles, State of California, shown outlined in red on the plat hereto attached and herein for convenience called "the subject property"; and

WHEREAS, Club desires to acquire from Filiorum and Filiorum is willing to grant to Club, but only during the continuance of the lease herein provided for, a non-exclusive license for ingress and egress over and across the property outlined in green on the attached plat and herein for convenience called "the access property", all subject to covenants and conditions herein set forth;

Now, therefore, in consideration of the promises and the agreements herein contained, the parties agree together as follows:

(1) Filiorum hereby leases to Club and Club leases from Filiorum, subject to termination, for rental and on covenants and conditions as herein set forth, the subject property for a term commencing on April 1, 1971 and ending March 31, 1974.

### Page 2

(2) Filiorum grants to Club a non-exclusive license for ingress and egress over and across the access property, such license to continue in effect only during the term of the lease herein provided for and to cease and terminate forthwith upon any termination of said lease.

(3) Club shall pay Filiorum as rental for the subject property the sum of $30,000 a Lease year for each lease year, or fraction thereof, of the term of this lease during which this lease continues in existence. Each "lease year", as the term is herein used, shall commence on April 1st of one calendar year and end on March 31st of the next succeeding calendar year. Such rental for each lease year shall be paid as follows: 60% of the income received by the Club from dues, beginning on the 10th day of April of first year, and on the 10th day of each month following for duration of lease, but in no event a total of less than $30,000. for each lease year. Should the Club incur expenses of an emergency character, it may make application to Filiorum and Filiorum may, in its sole discretion, reduce the rental for the lease year in which such emergency expenses are incurred to such amount as Filiorum may determine, but such a reduction shall not reduce the rental hereinabove provided for any subsequent lease year. Club agrees that its initiation fees, dues and assessments for each Club member for each lease year shall be a minimum of $150.

(4) The Club shall deposit all dues, initiation fees and other income received by it in a bank account in its name from which withdrawals may not be made without the signature of H. E. Benedict, or such other person as Filiorum may designate. Upon termination of this lease, performance of all obligations of Club

(6) Lessee, in exercising its rights hereunder, shall comply fully with all applicable laws, regulations and ordinances.

(7) Lessee shall keep the premises in good order and repair and in a neat and clean condition. Upon the expiration of any sooner termination of this lease, Lessee will surrender the premises to Lessor in a neat and clean condition and in as good a condition as the same now are, reasonable use and wear thereof excepted.

(8) Club shall secure, at its cost and expense, public liability insurance in form and with companies acceptable to Filiorum and with limits of $500,000 and $1,000,000, such insurance to be written for the benefit of Filiorum and Club and to insure Filiorum and Club against any and all claims for injury to or death of a person or persons, or loss or damage to property arising out of, resulting from or in any manner connected with the condition of the subject property or the access property or any operations conducted thereon by Club or any of its officers, agents, members or employees.

(9) Any notice to either party hereto shall be in writing. Any notice to Lessor may be addressed to Filiorum Corporation at Three Peppertree Drive, Portuguese Bend, California 90274, or at such other address as Filiorum may from time to time designate. Any notice to Club may be addressed to Abalone Shore Club at Post Office #2, Portuguese Bend, California 90274, or at such other address as Club may from time to time designate. Any notice herein provided for shall by conclusively deemed to have been given when the same is placed in a sealed envelope with postage thereon fully prepaid and addressed to the party for whom the same is intended, as above provided, and deposited in the United States mail.

### Page 5

(10) Lessee shall use the premises only for the purpose of conducting thereon a recreational beach club including, without limitation, the serving of food to its members and their guests and the furnishing of lifeguard service and swimming facilities.

(11) In the event of any default by Club in the payment of rental or in the performance of any of its obligations herein contained, and such default continues for seven days after notice to Club to remedy the same, Filiorum may, in addition to all its rights and remedies provided by law and without further notice to Club, terminate this lease and license and re-enter and take possession of the premises, using all necessary force so to do.

(12) Filiorum and its authorized representatives and public officials and authorized agents of Public Utilities may enter the premises at all times for inspection thereof and for other purposes not in conflict with Club's operations hereunder. In this connection, it is anticipated that the Club will install an electric gate across the access property at the approximate location shown on the attached plat. Club may do this provided it furnishes representatives of Filiorum and public officials designated by Filiorum with gate keys so that they may have access to the premises for the purposes above stated.

(13) Club may remove, prior to any termination of this lease, any and all improvements placed on the premises by it or for its account even though the same may be regarded as fixtures, including, without limitation, electric gates, barbeque pits, gang planks, floats, beach equipment, etc.
