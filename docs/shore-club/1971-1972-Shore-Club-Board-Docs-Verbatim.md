## Document 3: Letter to Mr. and Mrs. William Mayhall — August 24, 1972

[Abalone Shore Club letterhead with logo]

**OFFICERS**
Schuyler V. V. Hoffman, Jr., Chairman — 1963-71
Richard F. Karshner, President
Richard W. Park, Vice President
Thomas A. Spear, Vice President

Abalone Shore Club
P. O. Box #2308 — Palos Verdes Peninsula, California 90274

August 24, 1972

Re: Request for Function
on September 9, 1972

Mr. and Mrs. William Mayhall
32306 Phantom Drive
Palos Verdes Peninsula, California 90274

Dear Mr. and Mrs. Mayhall:

Reference is made to your July 19th letter, requesting permission for a function for a group of 24 adults (including 6 members). The Board of Directors, quite frankly, is basically unreceptive to your request in view of the Club rules which state:

"SPECIAL CLUB RULES

1. No large parties involving non-member guests will be permitted during the summer season while family participation is at its height. (Summer season being: Memorial Day to 3rd Sunday in September.)

RULES APPLYING BEFORE AND AFTER SUMMER SEASON

2. Rules applying before and after summer season as defined above:

A. Activities of an individual member involving 10 or more guests or joint member activities (including youth groups) involving 20 or more persons will be considered under the following conditions:

(a) Application in writing be made well in advance to the Board of Directors, stating sponsoring

---

[Page 2]

Mr. and Mrs. William Mayhall — 2

member, size of group, composition, and purpose of meeting, facilities required and arrangement for clean-up and admission of guests at the gate.

(b) No application will be considered for fund-raising activities or where there is an admission charge."

As the summer season does not end until Sunday, September 17th, we find that your request fails to meet the adopted Club Rules. However, because of our delay in replying, your function is approved.

You are aware that no Club facility can be reserved exclusively for any one group; approval is being given of another 24-person group function on the same date (September 9) as your event for the identical reason - delay in replying.

We are advising you that in the future requests for functions involving more than 10 non-member guests by an individual Club member or joint Club member functions involving more than a total of 10 non-member guests will not be approved during the summer season.

Very truly yours,

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

By ___K___
President

cc - Robert Edgerton
William Gardner
