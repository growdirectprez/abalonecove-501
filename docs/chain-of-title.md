# Chain of Title — Legal Lineage of the WPBCA Community

**Last updated:** 2026-03-26
**Purpose:** Trace the legal DNA of the community from the 1882 Bixby Partition through the current governing documents. Each node shows what the document does, what it adds or modifies, and what flows from it.

## What This Means

The legal chain runs unbroken from 1882 to the present. Palos Verdes Corporation created a comprehensive restriction scheme in 1949-1950 that covers two domains:

1. **Tract 14649** (81 lots) — Declaration No. One, restated in 2009
2. **Everything else PV Corp owned** — Lot "H" Declaration, auto-renewing, never restated

Together, these two declarations blanket the entire Portuguese Bend area in single-family residential restrictions. The only parcels outside this coverage are the 11 named tracts that had their own declarations (Rolling Hills, etc.) and land PV Corp did not own as of January 25, 1950.

APN 7573-006-024 (0 Clipper Road) is not in Tract 14649. It is not in any of the excepted tracts. It was PV Corp land in 1950. Therefore, it falls under the Lot "H" Declaration. Those restrictions auto-renewed on January 1, 2025.

No recorded release has been found.
