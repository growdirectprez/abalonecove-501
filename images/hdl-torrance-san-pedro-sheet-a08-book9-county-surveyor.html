Physical Description61 x 80 cm
DescriptionAn area of Los Angeles County in the southern part of Palos Verdes Peninsula, part of what is now Rancho Palos Verdes. The land is mostly agricultural, used for field and row crops, with large areas of vacant land in the northwest and along the coast in the southeast. The main east-west road is Palos Verdes Drive (which one is not specified). There are ms. notes just inside the neat line. Coordinates based on the location of Abalone Cove Ecological Preserve, just west of Portugese Point (on insert 1).
